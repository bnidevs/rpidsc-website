About Us

Developers Student Clubs is a program developed by Google developers to help the student community grow and learn, along with creating an inclusive environment for everyone eager to learn, open to both technical and non-technical majors.

What We Do

All members are welcome and encouraged to attend events including workshops about Google technologies, open to students of all skill levels looking for a collaborative environment.

Join Us

Looking to take part? Feel free to join the Discord at the link below!

Interested in joining the core team? Send us an email!

Technical leads head projects seeking solutions for issues in the community using Google technologies.

Non-technical leads connect members with the Google community, plan campus events, recruit new members, and film promotional videos.
